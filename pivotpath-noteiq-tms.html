The Training Lifecycle, End to End

Identify
Training Need Identification

Training items auto-create from approved DMS documents and role requirements, so every change surfaces who to train.

Create
Training Creation & Assignment

Courses are built from the source document and assigned to roles, with AI generating assessment questions.

Approve
Review & Approval

Content and assessments pass a controlled review-to-approve workflow before reaching learners.

Deliver
Training Delivery

Assigned learners receive courses with due dates; progress is tracked across teams.

Certify
Assessment & Certification

Learners complete the assessment; certification is recorded with audit trails and e-signatures.

Retrain
Retraining

When a document is revised or certification lapses, retraining triggers automatically to keep roles current.

What Quality & Training Leaders Ask About TMS

The end-to-end training lifecycle — need identification, creation, delivery, assessment, certification and retraining — unifying content, learners and performance.

When a document is approved or revised, TMS auto-creates and links the training, assigning people the latest version — with full traceability between document and record.

Instructor-led classroom training, e-learning and SCORM content, virtual sessions and on-the-job training — via role-based and event-driven assignment, self-paced or led.

AI-based assessment-question generation creates questions at training creation, so assessments build quickly and stay aligned with content.

Centralised records with completion tracking, audit trails, e-signatures and real-time dashboards — proving who is trained on what at inspection.
