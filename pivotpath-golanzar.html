Getting to know GoLanzar

Every launch function (regulatory, portfolio, finance, procurement, packaging, planning, business development, manufacturing) on one plan, so teams share the same milestones.

Yes. It replaces scattered spreadsheets and email with one accountable plan, so every function works from the same milestones and status is read from one source of truth, not rebuilt in meetings.

It maps every milestone and dependency onto the launch critical path and tracks them live, so a slipping milestone is visible and owned before it threatens the launch date.

Launch, portfolio and commercial leads, with dashboards and executive visibility across every function.

It’s purpose-built for pharmaceutical launches, with launch-specific functions, milestones and critical-path tracking, not a generic task tracker.
