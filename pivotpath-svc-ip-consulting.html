Built for the Decisions That Shape the Asset

When commitment is weeks away and clearance isn't

An infringement claim can freeze a funded launch. We clear freedom to operate with evidence across small molecules, biologics, nutraceuticals and medical devices, before the capital moves.

When the portfolio grows faster than the strategy

Coverage gaps open and spend scatters across filings that no longer match the pipeline. We manage families and territories to one plan, so the estate stays defensible.

When the science is proven but the deal isn't

A strong patent is not yet a route to market. We handle licensing, diligence and at-Risk launch documentation, drafted and negotiated for you.

When a Para IV or Patent Dance letter lands

The clock starts the day the letter arrives. We line up prior art, invalidity positions and deposition support before the dispute escalates, not assembled under deadline.
