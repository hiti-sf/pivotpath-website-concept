The Document Lifecycle, End to End

Set Up
Template Creation & Approval

Templates are authored, reviewed and approved, so every document starts compliant and standardised.

Author
Document Creation

Authors draft from a template or free-form, capturing structure and metadata from the outset.

Approve
Review & Approval

Documents move through configurable review and approval routes, every decision and signature audit-trailed.

Release
Release

Approved documents release immediately or on a delayed effective date, triggering event-based printing.

Maintain
Revision / Retire / Archive

Live documents are revised, retired or archived under version control; full history in the Virtual Data Room.
