Getting to know NoteIQ

One AI-enabled, modular platform: Validation Management (VMS), Document Management (DMS), Training Management (TMS) and Regulatory Intelligence (RegIntel) on one cloud-native system, compliance evidence connected.

Yes. Start with VMS, DMS, TMS or RegIntel and add the rest as you go, on the same platform.

Yes. Built for regulated, validated use with system-enforced controls: audit trails, e-signatures and role-based access on every module, aligned to 21 CFR Part 11, EU Annex 11, GAMP 5 and ALCOA+.

NoteIQ AI spans content intelligence (authoring, proofreading, summarisation), workflow automation (error/gap detection, deliverable and training-question generation) and decision support (tagging, plain-language regulatory summaries).

Yes. Unified across its modules and connected to ERP, QMS, LIMS and other enterprise applications.

AI here is assistive and human-reviewed — it drafts, summarises and retrieves, but a qualified person reviews and approves every regulated decision. Outputs are grounded in your approved content, fully audit-trailed, and your data is never used to train foundation models. See our AI Trust Centre for how we use AI responsibly.
