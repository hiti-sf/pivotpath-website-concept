Four Principles Behind Every AI Feature

Human-in-the-Loop

  • AI drafts, summarises and assists
  • People review, approve and can override
  • AI cannot approve, e-sign or finalise records
  • Configured workflow approvals stay in force

Grounded & Explainable

  • Retrieval-augmented from approved content
  • Controlled, task-specific prompts
  • Confidence scoring on responses
  • Answers trace back to source context

Traceable & Auditable

  • Every AI action can be logged
  • User, timestamp, input and output captured
  • Accept and reject decisions recorded
  • Aligned to your audit-trail framework

Private & Isolated

  • Logical tenant isolation on every layer
  • No data shared across customers
  • Customer content never trains models
  • Prompts and outputs stay in your boundary

How an AI Request Flows

Request
Request

A user asks the platform to draft, summarise or retrieve — inside a controlled, scope-restricted workflow.

Retrieval
Retrieve

The platform searches your approved documents, SOPs and records — meaning-based retrieval, not open-web knowledge.

Generation
Generate

A grounded draft is produced with a confidence indicator, citing the approved sources it drew on.

Human-led
Human Review

A qualified user checks the draft and its evidence, then accepts, edits or rejects it — nothing is automatic.

Recorded
Record

The approved output enters your workflow under configured approvals, with a full audit-trail entry.

AI supports the decision; the authorised user makes it.

Guardrails Against Hallucination & Error

Source Grounding

  • Retrieval-augmented from customer-approved documents
  • Document-grounded responses, not open-ended output
  • Prioritises your SOPs, templates and history

Controlled Prompts & Scope

  • Task-specific, controlled prompt templates
  • Scope-restricted AI interactions
  • The AI stays inside the task it was given

Confidence & Checkpoints

  • Confidence scoring and quality indicators
  • Human review checkpoints
  • Workflow-level approval controls

Accuracy Validation

  • Retrieval assessed on precision, recall & relevance
  • Generated output checked against source & business rules
  • Regression testing on AI-enabled features

Ongoing Monitoring

  • Usage trends and acceptance / rejection patterns
  • Prompt-effectiveness and retrieval-quality reviews
  • Model-version impact assessments

Correction & Change Control

  • Reject, correct or regenerate any output
  • Sources and configuration can be reassessed
  • Controlled change management with impact assessment

Where Your Data Lives, and How It's Protected

Tenant Isolation

  • Logical isolation across application, data, document, vector & access layers
  • No cross-tenant use of prompts, retrieved documents or outputs
  • Multi-tenant SaaS by default; dedicated VPC or private options where required

Data Residency

  • Hosted on AWS cloud infrastructure
  • Deployment in US, EU, India or other supported regions
  • Data not moved across regions without approval & planning

Encryption & Network

  • TLS 1.2+ encrypted communication
  • Encryption at rest where applicable
  • Security-group, firewall & optional IP-allowlisting controls

Access Control

  • Least-privilege, role-based access
  • Time-bound, logged administrative activity
  • Periodic access reviews aligned to your SOPs

Resilience

  • Automated backups & secure retention
  • Documented recovery & business-continuity procedures
  • Target recovery objectives on the order of ~1 hour, finalised per deployment

Model-Agnostic & Private AI

  • Supports OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude & AWS Bedrock models
  • Customer-approved private and local LLM deployments
  • Customer data is not used to train foundation models
