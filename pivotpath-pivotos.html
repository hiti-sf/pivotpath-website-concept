Governed at every layer.

The first questions about any new environment are about controls, not features. PivotOS carries four at every layer.

Audit trail

Recorded on create, change and delete, with user, timestamp and context. One framework across data, documents and AI.

Access and e-signatures

Least-privilege, role-based access and e-signatures under configured workflows, with periodic reviews aligned to your SOPs.

Monitoring and data quality

Platform health monitored continuously, data-quality checks in the pipelines, lineage kept from raw to refined.

Change control

Controlled change management with impact assessment and regression testing. Model and configuration changes are versioned.

PivotOS, Explained

We were our own first customer. Pivot Path grew out of a 35-year-old pharma group; our platforms carried our own pharmacovigilance, quality investigations and manufacturing IT before reaching a client. The environment built to hold that work became PivotOS.

No. You buy a platform or contract an operation, and the foundation comes with it. PivotOS is how we build and run our own products: one validated environment for data, AI and applications.

Engineered following GAMP 5 practices: change control, audit trails, electronic-records controls and role-based access. URS support, traceability and IQ/OQ artefacts support your validation; final responsibility depends on your intended use.
