How a Mobile Visit Runs

Initiate
Request & Consent

The investigator submits a service request and the patient provides informed consent for home-visit services.

Qualify
Qualify Local Provider

Local providers are identified, qualified and trained; credentials and CVs are reviewed and filed by the site.

Coordinate
Schedule & Supply

The provider contacts the patient to schedule the visit and receives laboratory kits and supplies from the site.

Deliver
On-Site Visit (Cold-Chain)

The visit is conducted at the patient’s location, with sample collection and shipment managed under cold-chain conditions.

Report
Document & Report

Source documents and results are shared with the site, and EDC entries and AE/SAE reporting are managed as per protocol.

Decentralised trials, your questions

Across 20+ trial sites in India, delivering decentralised home-health and on-site visits so patients can participate beyond major hospital sites while staying on protocol.

A team of 80+ GCP-trained study nurses and qualified local service providers, whose credentials, CVs and documentation are reviewed and filed by the investigator site before any visit.

Study drug administration (infusion, injection or topical), blood draws and other biologic sampling, clinical assessments (vitals, body weight, ECG, concomitant medications), patient training, compliance checks and patient-reported outcome questionnaires.

Samples are collected and shipped under cold-chain conditions, with laboratory kits and supplies coordinated from the site, so specimen integrity and timely reporting to the investigator site are maintained.

Source documents and laboratory results are shared with the investigator site and study coordinator, and all EDC entries and AE/SAE reporting are managed as per protocol under standardised SOPs.
