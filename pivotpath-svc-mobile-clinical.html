Built for the Realities of Hybrid Trials

When travel burden drives dropout

Distance quietly erodes enrolment and retention. We run visits at the patient's location, keeping eligible patients on study and their data on protocol.

When remote elements add to the site's load

Adding remote elements can bury a stretched site in logistics. We send field teams qualified, trained and supplied, that hand back complete documentation, so the site keeps oversight.

When a provider you didn't vet turns up

An unvetted provider at the bedside is a data-integrity and safety risk. We qualify every local provider and file their credentials with the site before the first visit.

When visit times suit the clinic, not the patient

Rigid clinic slots push patients to skip or reschedule visits. We schedule around the patient's week, so visits happen on protocol and the data stays complete.

How a Mobile Visit Runs

Initiate

The investigator submits a service request; the patient gives informed consent for home-visit services.

Qualify

Local providers are identified, qualified and trained — credentials and CVs reviewed and filed by the site.

Schedule

The visit is scheduled with the patient; laboratory kits and supplies are coordinated from the site.

Visit

Care is delivered at the patient's location, with samples collected and shipped under cold-chain conditions.

Report

Source documents reach the site and coordinator; EDC entries and AE/SAE reporting are managed per protocol.

One accountable field operation from request to report — the investigator site stays in control of every step.
