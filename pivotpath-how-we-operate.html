How we operate

Three ways of working across the pharma value chain: we consult, we operate, and we build the technology. Deepest where the work is most regulated.

Talk to our experts Explore our platforms

Consulting. Operations. Technology.

Consulting

Consulting when you need direction: IP strategy, regulatory pathways, operational excellence and audit readiness.

Operations

Operations when you want the work run for you: safety case processing, plant IT, quality systems and clinical operations, delivered to agreed service levels.

Technology

Our own software platforms underneath both, built for GxP environments and used in our own delivery before they reach clients. They run on PivotOS, with PivotAI as the intelligence layer.
