A Pharma Operations Platform company: seven software platforms, AI at the core, implemented and run by 450+ pharma-native operators. Born out of a 35-year-old pharma group, and built as a company seven years ago to turn operating knowledge into technology.

Five capability areas: patient safety, quality & compliance, digital transformation, integrated IP & clinical operations, and human capital, alongside software with AI at the core such as NoteIQ, NovaVigil, AnomIQ and InvestigationIQ.

They’re built for GxP environments (NoteIQ is 21 CFR Part 11 & EU Annex 11 compliant), and we validate using a risk-based CSA approach aligned to GAMP 5. Compliance is designed in, with full audit trails.

We operate to ISO 27001 and ISO 27701, certified for information security and privacy management. The same governance covers both the platforms we run and the operations we manage for you.

However it suits you: adopt a platform on its own, bring in a service team, or have us build and run the whole thing. The software and the people who operate it come from the same place.

Bring us the problem and we’ll scope the path, whether you’re comparing vendors, planning a transition, or ready to move. Talk to our experts and we’ll map it to the right capability or platform.
