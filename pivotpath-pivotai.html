PivotAI, Explained

No. PivotAI is the AI capability layer our platforms call on: retrieval, extraction, analysis and multi-step workflows running behind NovaVigil, NoteIQ, AnomIQ, InvestigationIQ and the rest. Where a conversational interface appears in a product, PivotAI is what answers, grounded in your approved content.

A model-agnostic architecture that supports leading enterprise providers, OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude and AWS Bedrock-hosted models, as well as customer-approved private and local LLM deployments. Model selection aligns with your security, compliance and data-residency requirements.

Agents carry multi-step tasks such as investigations, signal triage and document review across systems, but every agent runs with a human reviewer in the chain. AI cannot approve records, apply e-signatures or finalise workflows; every regulated decision stays with an authorised person.

PivotAI is delivered inside the platforms. You buy a platform, or contract an operation, and the AI layer comes with it, already integrated, already governed and already running on PivotOS.

No. Customer content is not used to train foundation models and stays within your own environment. The full data-handling and governance detail is in our AI Trust Centre.

PivotAI inherits PivotOS's validation posture and adds validation by design at the model level: outputs are versioned, traceable and re-runnable, so how a decision was reached can be shown on demand. Validation deliverables can be provided to support your own validation activities.
