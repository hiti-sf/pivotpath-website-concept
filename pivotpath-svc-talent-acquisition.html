When the milestone is fixed but the team isn't

A trial start or tech-transfer date does not move. Embedded recruiters scale the search to the deadline, so the date is met by the people who must deliver it.

When agencies send volume, not fit

Our recruiters screen with scientific literacy — they know pharmacovigilance from regulatory affairs and CSV from QA — so your scientists interview candidates, not CVs.
