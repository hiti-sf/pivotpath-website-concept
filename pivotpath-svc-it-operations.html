Built for the Realities of Manufacturing IT

When legacy systems fail without warning

A legacy system failing mid-shift can halt a production line for hours. We catch it with intelligent event correlation and automated remediation, and patch automation stops it recurring.

When the shop floor meets the network

Malware crossing from IT to the shop floor can stop production, not just email. We hold the boundary with secure IT-OT segregation, network segmentation and vulnerability management.

When the auditor asks who changed what

Reconstructing who touched a validated system can stall an inspection. We keep access changes traceable under governed access management, so the record answers the auditor first.

When routine work consumes the team

Your engineers lose their days to repetitive tickets, not improvement. We put routine process work under Robotics as a Service, so it runs hands-off and frees the team.
