InvestigationIQ, explained

An AI assistant for RCA and CAPA. It guides analysts through structured RCA grounded in your investigation history.

It ingests and chunks past investigations, then runs a similarity search for relevant prior cases — every suggestion anchored in your history.

Four RCA methods — Fishbone, 5-Why, 6M and Interviews — a structured, repeatable approach.

Yes. Human-in-the-loop by design — every output is reviewed before becoming a CAPA, keeping records defensible.

It targets an estimated 40–60% cut in cycle time — roughly 6–7 hours saved per investigation.

AI here is assistive and human-reviewed — it drafts, summarises and retrieves, but a qualified person reviews and approves every regulated decision. Outputs are grounded in your approved content, fully audit-trailed, and your data is never used to train foundation models. See our AI Trust Centre for how we use AI responsibly.
