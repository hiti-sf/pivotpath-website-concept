What Pharma 4.0 Actually Means

Connected Data Backbone

  • L0–L4 integration across the plant
  • Historian & MES as the system of record
  • OPC UA connectivity, edge to enterprise
  • One real-time picture of the plant

Golden-Batch Analytics

  • CMA + CPP + CCP = CQA modelling
  • PAT for in-line quality signals
  • Anomaly detection with AnomIQ
  • Command-room dashboards

Plug & Produce

  • Modular, reconfigurable equipment
  • MTP / OPC UA interoperability standards
  • Faster commissioning of new lines
  • Less custom integration effort

Validation 4.0

  • Risk-based CSA over check-box CSV
  • Data integrity by design
  • Validation that keeps pace with change
  • Less manual documentation burden

Continuous Process Verification

  • Real-time control strategy execution
  • Predictive maintenance on equipment
  • Drift caught before it becomes deviation
  • Live verification against the design space

Plant-Wide Digital Enablement

  • One operating model across sites
  • ICH Q10 pharmaceutical quality alignment
  • IT and OT teams working as one
  • People and process alongside the technology
