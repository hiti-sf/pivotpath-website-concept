Sync, match, stay accountable.

Three things, in order. InLumin syncs with the systems you already run, matches the paperwork automatically, and keeps every step accountable.

Sync

Picks up POs from your ERP (SAP HANA/ECC) and pulls the latest material spec from LIMS or your data warehouse, so the vendor always works to the current version.

Match

AI-driven 3-way matching across PO, ASN and GRN clears the routine automatically and flags the exceptions for a person.

Stay accountable

Role-based access, a full audit trail and a built-in Accounts Payable DMS, with request, approval and execution configured to your process.

Nothing critical is released without a human reviewer in the chain.

InLumin's 3-way matching runs on PivotAI; the platform is built and run on PivotOS, the validated GxP cloud foundation.

InLumin, Explained

InLumin is a procure-to-pay platform that runs alongside your ERP. It closes the loop from purchase request and PO through matching to payment, and brings your vendors onto the same platform.

No. It runs alongside your ERP as a companion system, picking up POs from it, so you close the procure-to-pay loop without starting another ERP project.

Yes. InLumin uses real-time ERP data integration, with SAP HANA/ECC as the named example, so there is no duplicate entry and one source of truth.

It matches the purchase order, the advance shipment note (ASN) and the goods receipt note (GRN). The routine clears automatically on PivotAI and exceptions are flagged for a person.

Vendors log in and upload their own shipment and invoice documents, and communications are automated. InLumin also pushes the latest material spec from LIMS or your data warehouse, so the vendor works to the current version.

Yes, as an extended capability. A vendor scorecard draws on regulatory signals such as FDA warning letters and Form 483 observations, and you can run configurable vendor qualification and requalification forms inside the platform.

Procurement and Supply Chain Management teams, and finance, through the built-in Accounts Payable DMS.

AI here is assistive and human-reviewed. It drafts, summarises and retrieves, but a qualified person reviews and approves every regulated decision. Outputs are grounded in your approved content, fully audit-trailed, and your data is never used to train foundation models.
