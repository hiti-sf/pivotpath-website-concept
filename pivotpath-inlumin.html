From PO to Payment, Matched and Logged

Initiate
PO Raised

A purchase order is created and given an owner, opening the cycle.

Notify
ASN Received

The supplier's advance shipment notice captures what's inbound.

Receive
GRN Logged

Goods received are recorded against the order, confirming what arrived.

Match
AI 3-Way Match

AI reconciles PO, ASN and GRN, flagging any discrepancy.

Resolve
Exception or Approve

Clean matches approve straight through; mismatches branch to exceptions.

Settle
Pay

Approved transactions move to payment, the matched record synced to SAP.
